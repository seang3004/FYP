People in Cluster 0 are the least likely people to view themselves as the life of the party preferring to be in the background of social situations prefferring to talk to the same few people or not really talking at all. They typically can be more prone to feelings of stress and worry than other people but rarely believe that they are feeling blue. They tend to get irritated and annoyed a lot more easily than other people. They typically can be interested in people but it tends to be people that they consider to be important to them. They tend to prioritise other things over doing chores and jobs leaving them until later. They will usually spend some time reflecting on the important things that have happened to them. They usually are quite imaginative being able to understand concepts that generally can be considered to be abstract. They will be more likely to give their time to those they feel deserve it.
